Your Privacy is Our Priority

This Privacy Policy governs the use of the software application "2D Expect" (the "Application") developed by Micheal Aung.


We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the Application.

1. Information Collection and Use

We may collect certain information automatically when you use the Application to provide and improve our services.

A. Automatically Collected Information

The Application collects the following types of data:

  • Usage Data (via Firebase Analytics):
    • Details about how you use the Application, such as the time and duration of your activity, the pages or screens you visit, and which features you interact with (e.g., viewing Live Results or Number Prediction data).
    • Information about your device, including the device type, operating system, and unique identifiers (e.g., Firebase Installation IDs).
    • Note: This data is collected in an aggregated and anonymous form and does not include personally identifiable information like your name, address, or phone number.
  • Notification Information (via Firebase Cloud Messaging):
    • We use your device's unique identifier (Device Token/Installation ID) to send you Push Notifications (such as real-time updates for SET Index results or important announcements).

B. Purposes of Use

The collected information is used for the following purposes:

  • To Provide and Maintain the Service: To ensure the core functions of the app (displaying Live Results, numbers) work correctly.
  • To Improve the Service: To analyze usage trends, fix bugs, and enhance the performance and design of the Application.
  • For Technical Support: To identify and resolve technical issues, crashes, or errors.
  • To Send Notifications: To deliver time-sensitive updates on the SET Index results and other relevant news via Push Notifications.

2. Information Sharing

We share your information only with the following Third-Party Service Providers:

Google Firebase (Analytics & Cloud Messaging):
Firebase is a service provided by Google that helps us develop, monitor, and improve our Application. They handle and process data according to their own Privacy Policy.

We will not share your personal data with any other parties except in the event that we are legally required to do so or if it is necessary to protect our rights and property.

3. Age Limitation

Our Service is not directed to anyone under the age of 13. By using the Application, you represent that you are at least 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

4. Your Choices (Notifications)

You have the option to enable or disable the Push Notifications from the Application at any time through your device's system settings.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
